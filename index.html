Score guide


/36 numbers: Varna 1, Vashya 2, Tara 3, Yoni 4, Graha Maitri 5, Gana 6, Bhakoot 7, Nadi 8 — traditionally the Moon (your mind/thoughts) is the biggest affector on marriage success, though other positions affect timing, karma in person chosen, whether the marriage has a strong love bond versus monetary or duty, etc.



Below 18 = below the usual suitability threshold, 18+ = meets the usual suitability threshold. The general rule is that <18 is inauspicious (gives bad luck) and above that is acceptable, with the higher you go, the easier the marriage will be as minds will be aligned. If you are a Hindu, this type of reading is done prior to every marriage as a custom.



Older Vedic and Gṛhya sources discuss auspicious nakshatras for marriage; the full 36-guna scoring framework is found in later Jyotish texts such as Jyotirnibandha and Muhūrtacintāmaṇi from AD 1500. Exact score labels vary across later sources but this an averaged score.
